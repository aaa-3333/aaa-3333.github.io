Harry Potter

Introduction

Original Introduction

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling. The novel tells the life of young wizard Harry Potter, and his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger , both students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry . The story revolves around the conflict between Harry and Voldemort .

The first book "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" , was pulished on June 26,1997, and became widely popular around the world. As of Feburaary 2023, over 600 million copies have been sold world wide in dozens of languages, making it the best-selling children's book series

Warner Bros. Pictures has adapted seven original books into eight film.In 2016, the total value of the Harry Potter series was estimated to be $25 billion, making it one of the highest grossing media series of all time.

Harry Potter Movies









Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001)

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first movie in the series, where Harry discovers he's a wizard and starts his magical journey at Hogwarts.










Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)

In the second film, Harry returns to Hogwarts and uncovers the mystery of the Chamber of Secrets, facing dangers that threaten the school.










Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)

In the third film, Harry learns about sirius Black, an escaped prisoner who might be connected to his family's past.










Harry Potter and the Goblet of the Fire (2005)

The fourth movie features the Triwizard Tournament, where Harry competes in a series of deadly magical tasks.












Harry Potter and the Order of the phoenix (2007)

Harry forms "Dumbledore's Army" to fight against the threat of Load Voldemort, while facing the Ministry of Magic's opposition.











Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009)

In the sixth movie, Harry learns about Voldemort's past, and a crucial plan to defeat him is set in motion.










Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 (2010)

The first part of the final movie, where Harry, Ron, and Hermione set off to find and destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes.









Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (2011)

The final battle between Harry and Voldemort culminates in this epic conclusion to the series, where Harry must destroy the Dark Lord once and for all.
